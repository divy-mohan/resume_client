Refund Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2025

Your satisfaction is our priority. Learn about our refund and cancellation policies.

1. Our Satisfaction Guarantee

At Professional Writers, we stand behind the quality of our work. We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee on all our services. If you're not completely satisfied with your career document, we'll make it right.

100% Satisfaction Guarantee

We're committed to delivering exceptional quality. If you're not satisfied, we'll refund your money within 7 days of delivery.

2. Refund Eligibility

You are eligible for a full refund under the following conditions:

2.1 Quality Issues

  • The delivered document does not meet the specifications discussed
  • Significant errors in content, formatting, or presentation
  • Document fails to address your career level or industry requirements
  • ATS optimization standards not met as promised

2.2 Service Issues

  • Failure to deliver within the promised timeframe (excluding delays caused by client)
  • Lack of response from our team for more than 48 hours during working days
  • Service not provided as described in the package details

2.3 Timeline Requirements

  • Refund requests must be made within 7 days of delivery
  • You must provide specific reasons for dissatisfaction
  • Allow us the opportunity to address concerns through revisions first

3. Refund Process

Our refund process is designed to be fair and transparent:

1
Submit Request

Contact our support team with your order details and specific concerns about the delivered work.

2
Review Process

Our team will review your case within 24-48 hours and attempt to resolve issues through revisions.

3
Decision & Processing

If revisions don't meet your satisfaction, we'll approve the refund and process it within 5-7 business days.

4
Payment Return

Refunds are processed to the original payment method. Bank transfers may take additional 3-5 business days.

4. Partial Refunds

In certain situations, we may offer partial refunds:

  • Bundled Services: If only part of a bundle doesn't meet expectations
  • Rush Orders: If expedited delivery wasn't met, rush charges may be refunded
  • Add-on Services: Specific add-ons that weren't delivered as promised
  • Scope Changes: When client requirements change mid-project

5. Non-Refundable Situations

While we strive to accommodate all reasonable requests, refunds may not be available in these situations:

5.1 Client-Related Issues

  • Failure to provide necessary information or feedback
  • Changes in requirements after project completion
  • Requests beyond the agreed scope of work
  • Dissatisfaction with content accuracy when client provided incorrect information

5.2 Timeline Issues

  • Refund requests made after the 7-day window
  • Services already used or implemented successfully
  • Documents that have been shared or used for job applications

5.3 Subjective Preferences

  • Style or format preferences not specified initially
  • Personal taste differences on design elements
  • Changes in career goals after completion

6. Cancellation Policy

6.1 Before Work Begins

You can cancel your order and receive a full refund if:

  • Cancellation is requested within 24 hours of placing the order
  • No consultation or writing work has commenced
  • No information has been shared with the assigned writer

6.2 Work in Progress

If work has already begun:

  • Consultation fees may be deducted from the refund
  • Partial work completed will be charged proportionally
  • Minimum charges may apply based on the stage of completion

6.3 Rush Orders

Rush orders have different cancellation terms:

  • No cancellation allowed once work begins due to priority allocation
  • Rush charges are non-refundable if cancellation occurs after writer assignment

7. Refund Methods and Timeline

7.1 Payment Method Returns

Refunds are processed to the original payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-7 business days
  • UPI/Net Banking: 3-5 business days
  • PayPal: 1-3 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 7-10 business days

7.2 Processing Timeline

Our internal processing timeline:

  • Request acknowledgment: Within 24 hours
  • Case review: 24-48 hours
  • Decision communication: Within 72 hours
  • Refund initiation: Within 5 business days of approval

8. Revision vs. Refund

Before processing a refund, we encourage utilizing our revision process:

Why Choose Revisions First?
  • Unlimited revisions during development stage
  • Direct communication with your assigned writer
  • Faster resolution than refund processing
  • Opportunity to achieve your exact requirements
  • Maintain your 2-year support benefits
